Privacy Policy — LLMSchool

Last updated: May 3, 2026

1. Overview

LLMSchool ("the App") is a personal-use desktop application. This Privacy Policy explains what data the App accesses and how it is handled.

2. Information We Collect

The App accesses only the TikTok account of its single owner via TikTok's official OAuth 2.0 authorization flow. Specifically, the App requests the following scopes:

  • user.info.basic — to display the connected account's public username and avatar inside the App as a confirmation that the correct account is linked.
  • video.upload — to upload videos to the owner's own TikTok drafts via TikTok's Content Posting API.

The App does not collect, store, or process data from any user other than its owner.

3. How We Use Information

Authentication tokens issued by TikTok are stored locally on the owner's machine in a permission-restricted file. They are used solely to authenticate API calls when uploading videos to the owner's own TikTok account.

4. Data Sharing

No data is shared with any third party. The App has no backend server; all API calls are made directly from the owner's local machine to TikTok's official endpoints.

5. Data Retention

Tokens are retained locally until the owner manually deletes them or revokes access from TikTok account settings. Uploaded videos are managed entirely within the owner's TikTok account under TikTok's own privacy terms.

6. Security

Authentication tokens are stored in a local file with restricted file-system permissions on the owner's device. The App does not transmit tokens to any server other than TikTok's official API endpoints.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The latest version will always be available at this URL.
